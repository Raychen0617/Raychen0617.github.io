Dual‑branch Cross‑Patch Attention Learning for Group Affect Recognition

- The work is submitted to CVPR 2023.
- In this work, we proposed a dual cross‑attention vision transformer framework for group affect recognition.
- We designed a two‑branch network based on the vision transformer. Each branch focused on different aspects of the image: One focused on the overall background; the other focused on the most important person’s emotion in the image.
- Our neural network learned to drop insignificant image tokens to increase its efficiency.
- With parameters less than 10x, our method outperformed state‑of‑the‑art methods on group‑affect datasets.
